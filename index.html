What is it?

nujjReceipts is an app that gets rid of the pesky paper receipts cluttering up your wallets and purses.

We think paper receipts are annoying, so we came up with a way to turn them into a digital version on your phone that you can use in store when returning purchases.

The app notifies you when the returns period for your items is about to expire so you don't forget.

You can back up your nujjReceipts to your cloud service as well (iCloud/Google Drive), so you never lose a receipt again.

Your receipts, in one place, on your phone.

How Does it Work?

Step 1
Download the Beta by signing up, above.


Step 2
Add a receipt to the app by scanning and imaging your paper receipt.


Step 3
Use the receipt in store as proof of purchase when returning items.


That’s it!
